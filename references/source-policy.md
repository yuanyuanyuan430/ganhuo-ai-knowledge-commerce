# Source Policy

This skill improves commercial execution by making sources cleaner, more traceable, and less likely to create account or trust problems.

## Preferred Source Types

| Source type | Use | Notes |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Owned experience | Core content, cases, proof | Most valuable and hardest to copy |
| Authorized material | Paid products, partner content, interviews | Keep written permission and scope |
| Official documentation | Tool tutorials, platform rules, technical explanations | Re-check dates before publishing |
| Public reports | Market context, statistics, trend claims | Cite source and date |
| Open-license content | Background and examples | Check BY, SA, NC, ND restrictions |
| User-submitted cases | Case studies, member wins | Get consent and anonymize when useful |
| Tool tests | Screenshots, comparisons, workflows | Strong trust proof |

## Prohibited Or Restricted Source Rules

Do not use these as commercial source material unless the operator has clear written rights and a defensible reason:

- paid community exports without permission;
- course backend screenshots or member-only pages without permission;
- group chat logs without consent from the people involved;
- full article dumps or creator archives;
- copied tables, templates, or cases from a single creator;
- user reviews, income screenshots, or private data without permission;
- material advertised as internal, exclusive, leaked, or cracked.

If a source is public on the web, still check the site's terms, robots guidance, copyright notice, license, and attribution requirements. Public visibility does not automatically mean commercial reuse is allowed.

## Rewrite Rule

AI rewriting improves readability, not ownership. Improve the content by adding:

- original testing;
- user-specific framing;
- lived experience;
- new examples;
- templates created for this product;
- explicit source links;
- practical limits and failure cases.

## Sales Claim Rule

Prefer claim shapes that can be proven:

- "提高选题和发布效率" instead of "自动赚钱";
- "跑通首个付费闭环" instead of "保证收入";
- "降低试错成本" instead of "稳赚";
- "适合愿意每周投入时间的人" instead of "躺赚".
